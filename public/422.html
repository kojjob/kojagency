422

Unprocessable Entity

The server understands your request but cannot process it due to validation errors. The data you submitted may be incomplete, invalid, or violates business rules.

Common Causes
  • Missing required fields in a form
  • Invalid data format or type
  • Business rule violations
  • Authorization or permission issues
  • CSRF token mismatch or expiration
